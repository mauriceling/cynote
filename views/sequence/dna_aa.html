Basic Sequence Utilities


This page contains tools to perform basic sequence manipulations:
1. Complementation: Produce the complementary sequence of the given DNA sequence (anti-sense strand from sense strand)
2. Transcription: Transcribes the given DNA sequence to RNA
3. Translation: Translates the given DNA/RNA sequence in amino acid sequence
4. Back Transcription: Produce the DNA sequence from given RNA sequence
